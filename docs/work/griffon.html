Griffon Hoverwork

Starting in 2014, I spent twelve months working for Griffon Hoverwork.


During this period, I worked in two of the main departments within their Design Office; Modelling, where detailed designs of components and assemblies to go on the craft were created, and Statutory Compliance, the department that made sure all designs that were approved met the standards that the MCA (Maritime Coastguard Agency) and HSC (High Speed Craft) codes stated. I had the opportunity to work on a wide variety of projects, including designing many components for the new 12000TD, as well as producing and submitting various schematics to the MCA for approval.


This report summarizes some of the key projects and tasks I completed during my time with the company.
