IMAGE INSTRUCTIONS

For posts;

* Hero images must have an aspect ratio of 16:9 
* Content images can be any size, but;
  - ideally will have an aspect ratio of 16:9,
  - if two or more images are used, their aspect ration must be the same

* Thumbnails MUST have a 16:9 ratio.
  - The ideal size is 200 x 113.

* The maximum width of images are;
  - HERO: 1920 x 1080
  - CONTENT: 1080 x 608
  - THUMBNAILS: 200 x 113
