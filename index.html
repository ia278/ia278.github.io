I'm Isaac Anderson

a recent mechanical design engineering graduate now moving into UX design and web development.

But why move? Engineering's cool, right?

It is. And being able to design and build physical products is something I've enjoyed learning for the past five years. But using psychology to understand why people make certain decisions, and using this knowledge to design and build digital products that people like using? Perfect.

Of course, I'm not doing this 24/7. You'll probably find me on a tennis court if it's sunny, or planning a holiday to sunnier places if it's not.

Anyway, enough writing about me. Feel free to check out some of my work below, or look at my resume and GitHub repository. And if you like what you see - get in contact!
